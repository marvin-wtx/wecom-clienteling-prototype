# Brand Expression, After Operating Truth

Creative direction is optional expression, not a workflow-selection mechanism.

Once the daily jobs work, use brand evidence to choose tone, density, type scale, imagery restraint, and component emphasis. A strong prototype may have a distinct visual anchor or information rhythm, but it may not rename ordinary work into an invented business system or move work out of the first viewport to support a concept.

Use outside inspiration only for transferable interface mechanisms (grouping, disclosure, progress, recovery, hierarchy). Do not treat it as evidence for business terminology or operating logic.

When comparing prior cases, compare actual jobs and interaction paths only when there is a real prior case. Do not force artificial divergence from a generic shell or starter demo.
